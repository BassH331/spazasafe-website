The Crisis

COUNTERFEIT FOOD IS
KILLING OUR CHILDREN

In 2024, South Africa faced a devastating food poisoning crisis. Counterfeit products infiltrated our communities, with tragic consequences that demand immediate action.

  • Fake cooking oil containing industrial lubricants sold to unsuspecting families
  • Counterfeit baby formula with dangerous chemical substitutes
  • Expired products repackaged with falsified expiration dates
  • Brand imitations using toxic inks and unsafe plastic packaging
  • No verification tools available at point of purchase
Our Solution

AI VERIFICATION IN YOUR POCKET

SpazaSafe combines advanced computer vision with deep learning to detect counterfeit products in seconds. Simply scan, capture, and know.

Features

BUILT FOR REAL-WORLD PROTECTION

VISUAL FORENSICS

ResNet18 deep learning analyzes packaging structure and design elements that counterfeiters cannot perfectly replicate.

GEOMETRY ANALYSIS

ORB keypoint detection identifies spatial distortions and logo placement errors invisible to the human eye.

COLOR VERIFICATION

HSV histogram comparison detects subtle ink and printing deviations that reveal counterfeit production.

MOBILE-FIRST DESIGN

Optimized for low-bandwidth environments and older devices commonly used in township communities.

SUB-SECOND RESULTS

Get instant verification with confidence scores and actionable guidance before you buy.
